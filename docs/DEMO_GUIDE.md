# Demo GIF Guide

## 🎬 Step-by-Step Recording Script
To record a perfect 20-30 second demo GIF of BlackBoard, follow this script:

1. **Setup**: Open two browser windows (e.g., Chrome and Firefox, or normal/incognito) and snap them side-by-side on your screen.
2. **Action 1 (0:00 - 0:08)**: Select the pen tool on Window A. Draw a shape or write "Hello". Watch it appear instantly on Window B.
3. **Action 2 (0:08 - 0:15)**: On Window B, click the Sticky Note tool. Drop a note on the canvas, type "Great idea!", and move the note around. Watch it sync back to Window A.
4. **Action 3 (0:15 - 0:22)**: Select the Text tool on Window A. Click the canvas and type "Project Goals". Select the text and edit it.
5. **Action 4 (0:22 - 0:30)**: Quickly press `Ctrl+Z` (Undo) a few times to watch the text and sticky note disappear, then `Ctrl+Y` (Redo) to bring them back.

## 🛠 Recommended Free Tools

### macOS
**[Kap](https://getkap.co/)**
- Open-source, lightweight, and specifically designed for screen recording to GIF/MP4.
- **How to use**: Select your recording area (crop exactly to your two browser windows), hit record, execute the script, and export directly as a high-quality GIF.

### Windows
**[ScreenToGif](https://www.screentogif.com/)**
- Free, open-source, and extremely powerful editor.
- **How to use**: Open the app, click "Recorder", frame your two browsers within the translucent window, click Record, follow the script, and click Stop. You can trim unnecessary frames before saving as a GIF!
